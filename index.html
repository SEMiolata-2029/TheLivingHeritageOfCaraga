Welcome to Caraga's Cultural Tapestry

The Caraga Region in northeastern Mindanao, Philippines, is a land rich with cultural diversity and indigenous heritage. Home to six major indigenous tribes, this region showcases centuries of tradition, wisdom, and sustainable living practices that continue to thrive today.

Each tribe carries unique customs, languages, and artistic expressions that contribute to the vibrant cultural mosaic of Caraga. From the mountain-dwelling Manobo to the ancient Mamanwa, these communities preserve invaluable knowledge about living in harmony with nature.
